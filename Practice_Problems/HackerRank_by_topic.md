# HackerRank Practice — by topic

HackerRank is what many service companies actually use for their online tests, so its style
matters. These are from the **Interview Preparation Kit** and **Problem Solving** track —
heavy on input parsing and warm-up logic, which is exactly your Day-1 I/O skill.

> Workflow: these read from STDIN and print to STDOUT. Practice `input()`,
> `int(input())`, and `map(int, input().split())` — that parsing is half the battle on HR.

### Suggested order
Do all of section 1 first — it teaches HackerRank's input/output format, which trips people
up more than the logic does. Then sections 2–4 in any order. The ⭐ Mediums (Sparse Arrays,
New Year Chaos) are good once the Easies feel routine.

**Tip:** On HackerRank, follow the **"3 Months Preparation Kit"** and **"1 Week Preparation
Kit"** — they bundle most of the above in a guided plan with a progress bar.
